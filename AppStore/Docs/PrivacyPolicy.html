Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 29, 2026

1. Introduction

VitaMind ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how VitaMind collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our iOS application (the "App").

By downloading or using the VitaMind App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. Please read it carefully.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Health Data

VitaMind integrates with Apple HealthKit to collect and process the following health data:

  • Heart rate and heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Sleep analysis data (total sleep, deep sleep, REM sleep)
  • Step count and physical activity data
  • Exercise minutes and workout data
  • Blood oxygen saturation (SpO2)
  • Body temperature measurements
  • Stand hours and movement data

2.2 Usage Data

We may collect non-personal information about how you interact with the App, including:

  • Features accessed and usage patterns
  • Device type and iOS version
  • App crashes and performance data
  • Anonymous usage analytics

2.3 AI Conversation Data

When you interact with the VitaMind AI assistant, the conversation content is processed to provide AI-powered health insights. These conversations are processed on-device using Apple's CoreML technology to protect your privacy.

🔒 Privacy First: All health data collected through VitaMind stays on your device. We use on-device AI processing via Apple CoreML, ensuring your most sensitive health information never leaves your phone.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide personalized health insights and recommendations
  • Sync health data with Apple Watch and HealthKit
  • Track your habits and send smart reminders
  • Generate health reports and trend analysis
  • Improve our App's features and user experience
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries

4. Data Storage and Security

Your health data is stored securely on your iOS device using Apple's secure enclave. VitaMind implements the following security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmissions
  • On-device AI processing (CoreML) for sensitive health data
  • Secure data storage using iOS Keychain and HealthKit encryption
  • Regular security updates and vulnerability patches

5. Data Sharing

VitaMind does not sell, trade, or rent your personal health information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for research or analytics purposes, but this data cannot be used to identify you.

We may share your information with:

  • Apple (for HealthKit integration and CloudKit sync)
  • Service providers who assist in operating our App
  • Legal authorities when required by law

6. Apple HealthKit Permissions

VitaMind requests access to Apple HealthKit for the following purposes:

  • Read: Heart rate, sleep, steps, workouts, and related health metrics
  • Write: Habit completion data and health insights
  • Background Delivery: Real-time sync of health data changes

You can revoke HealthKit access at any time through iOS Settings > Privacy > HealthKit.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal health data stored in VitaMind
  • Export your data in machine-readable format (PDF, CSV)
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Control notification preferences
  • Revoke HealthKit permissions at any time

🗑️ Data Deletion: To delete your VitaMind account and all data, go to Settings > Privacy > VitaMind > Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data from our servers and devices.

8. Children's Privacy

VitaMind is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Your continued use of VitaMind after any modifications indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
